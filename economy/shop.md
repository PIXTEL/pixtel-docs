# Shop

The PIXTEL Shop is where players buy items, cosmetics, and currency packages using XIPS or real money.

## Accessing the Shop

Open the Shop from the main menu. Items are organized into categories with tabs for easy browsing.

## Product Types

### Furniture & Items

Decorative objects for your room:
- Tables, chairs, sofas, beds
- Themed items (bar, casino, gym, haunted)
- Special objects (arcade machines, pianos, jacuzzis)
- Seasonal items (holiday specials)

Each item has:
- A **preview image**
- **Price** in XIPS
- **Size** (grid dimensions for placement)
- **Stock** (some items are limited)

### Pool Cues

Customize your pool game with different cue designs:
- The **default cue** is free for everyone
- Premium cues are available in the shop
- Cues are purely cosmetic — they don't affect gameplay
- Once purchased, a cue is permanently added to your collection

### XIPS Packages

Buy XIPS directly:
- Multiple package sizes available
- Purchased with real money through the Solana integration
- XIPS are credited instantly after purchase confirmation

### Rooms

Unlock room templates and special room features through the shop.

## Purchase Flow

1. **Browse** the shop and select an item
2. **Click Buy** to initiate the purchase
3. **Confirm** the transaction
4. The item is added to your **inventory** immediately
5. Your XIPS balance updates in real time

## Inventory

After purchasing:
- **Furniture** appears in your room decoration inventory
- **Cues** appear in the cue rack during pool games
- **XIPS** are added directly to your balance

## Duplicate Protection

The shop prevents accidental duplicate purchases:
- If you already own a pool cue and try to buy it again, you'll see a warning: "You already own this cue!"
- Furniture items can be purchased multiple times (you might want several of the same chair)

## Stock System

Some items have **limited stock**:
- Stock decreases with each purchase
- When stock reaches 0, the item becomes unavailable
- Items with unlimited stock show no stock indicator

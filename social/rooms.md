# Rooms & Hotel

The PIXTEL hotel is the central hub of the game. It contains public community spaces and player-created rooms where all social interaction happens.

## Public Rooms

These are permanent rooms that everyone can access:

| Room | Theme | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Street | Outdoor hub | The main entrance area, where players first arrive |
| Lobby | Hotel lobby | Central meeting point with furniture and tables |
| Bar | Nightlife | A social drinking spot with bar furniture |
| Casino | Gambling | Decorated with slot machines and gaming tables |
| Gym | Fitness | Workout-themed with gym equipment |
| Disco | Party | Dance floor with lighting effects |
| Classroom | School | Educational theme with desks and boards |

Public rooms are always available and can hold multiple players at the same time.

## Player-Created Rooms

Any player can create their own room:

### Room Properties

- **Name** — Give your room a custom name
- **Visibility** — Public (anyone can join) or Private (invite only)
- **Size** — Small, Medium, or Large (affects the floor area and maximum furniture)
- **Icon** — Choose a theme icon for your room in the room list

### Creating a Room

1. Open the **Rooms** panel
2. Click **Create Room**
3. Set the name, visibility, and size
4. Your room appears in the room list

### Room Restrictions

- **Guest users** (not logged in) can only create private rooms
- **Wallet users** holding the PIXTEL token can create public rooms
- **Non-holders** are restricted from creating public rooms

### Managing Your Room

As the room owner, you can:
- **Decorate** with furniture from your inventory
- **Delete** the room (only when it's empty)
- View current player count

## Navigating Between Rooms

- Use the **Room List** panel to see all available rooms
- Click on any room to enter it
- Your character will appear in the new room
- Your presence status updates automatically so friends can see where you are

## Room Features

Each room supports:
- **Real-time multiplayer** — see other players walking around
- **Room chat** — public messages visible to everyone in the room
- **Minigame tables** — walk up to a table to start or join a minigame
- **Furniture interaction** — placed objects are visible to all players

# Furniture & Decoration

Player-created rooms in PIXTEL can be decorated with furniture and objects purchased from the shop.

## How It Works

1. **Buy furniture** from the [Shop](../economy/shop.md)
2. **Enter your room** and open the decoration mode
3. **Place objects** by dragging them from your inventory onto the room
4. **Rotate** items to fit your layout
5. All placed furniture is **visible to every player** who enters your room

## Placement System

Furniture uses a **grid-based placement system**:

- Each item has a **size** (width x height in grid cells)
- Items snap to the grid when placed
- **Collision detection** prevents overlapping objects
- Some items support **rotation** (90-degree increments)
- Items have a **height level** for visual layering

## Item Categories

The shop offers furniture across different themes:

- **Bar** — counters, stools, shelves, bottles
- **Casino** — slot machines, card tables, chips
- **Bedroom** — beds, wardrobes, nightstands
- **Gym** — dumbbells, benches, mats
- **Haunted** — spooky decorations, cobwebs
- **General** — sofas, tables, chairs, plants, rugs
- **Special** — unique items like arcade machines, pianos, guitars, jacuzzis

## Inventory

Your furniture inventory tracks:
- **Item name** and visual preview
- **Quantity** owned
- **Placement status** — whether the item is placed in a room or in storage

New accounts receive a set of **starter items** to begin decorating immediately.

## Seasonal Items

Special furniture is available during events:
- Holiday-themed decorations (Christmas, Halloween)
- Limited-time items that may not return
- Season tags track when items were distributed

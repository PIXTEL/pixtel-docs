# Friends & Chat

PIXTEL has a full social system for connecting with other players.

## Friends

### Adding Friends

1. Open the **Friends** panel
2. Search for a player by their nickname or ID
3. Send a **friend request**
4. Wait for them to accept

### Friend List

Your friend list shows:
- **Online/offline status** — see who's currently playing
- **Current room** — see which room your online friends are in
- **Quick actions** — send a message or visit their room

### Friend Requests

- **Pending** requests show in a separate tab
- You can **accept** or **reject** incoming requests
- You can **cancel** outgoing requests
- A notification counter shows unread requests

## Chat

### Room Chat

Every room has a **public chat channel**:

- Messages are visible to all players in the room
- Your nickname and character are shown with each message
- Chat history loads when you enter a room
- New messages appear in real time

### Direct Messages (DMs)

For private conversations:

1. Open the **DM** panel (or click a friend's profile)
2. Select a conversation or start a new one
3. Type your message and send
4. Messages are private between you and the other player

DM features:
- **Message history** — scroll through past conversations
- **Unread counter** — see how many unread messages you have
- **Conversation list** — sorted by most recent message
- **Read receipts** — conversations are marked as read when opened

## Presence

PIXTEL tracks player presence in real time:

- Your **online status** is visible to friends
- Your **current scene** (which room you're in) is shared
- Friends can see when you're in a game or exploring rooms
- Status updates automatically when you change rooms

## Nicknames

- Set your nickname from the **Profile** panel
- Nicknames are visible in chat, game tables, and the friends list
- Names are validated (no special characters or inappropriate content)
- You can change your nickname at any time

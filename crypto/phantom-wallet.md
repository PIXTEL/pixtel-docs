# Phantom Wallet

[Phantom](https://phantom.app) is the primary wallet supported by PIXTEL. It's a browser extension that lets you interact with the Solana blockchain.

## Setting Up Phantom

1. **Install Phantom** — Visit [phantom.app](https://phantom.app) and install the browser extension
2. **Create or import a wallet** — Follow Phantom's setup to create a new wallet or import an existing one
3. **Fund your wallet** — Add SOL for transaction fees and optionally buy PIXTEL tokens

## Connecting to PIXTEL

1. Go to [play.pixtel.fun](https://play.pixtel.fun)
2. On the login screen, choose **Connect Wallet**
3. Phantom will open a popup asking you to approve the connection
4. Once connected, your account is linked to your Solana address

## What You Can Do With Phantom

### Sign In

Your Phantom wallet serves as your identity. No email or password needed — just your wallet.

### View Balance

Your wallet balance (SOL + PIXTEL tokens) is displayed in the game UI. The balance refreshes automatically with retry logic if the connection is slow.

### Approve Transactions

When you:
- **Buy items** from the shop
- **Burn tokens** at the Fireplace
- **Swap tokens** for XIPS

Phantom will open a popup asking you to sign the transaction. You always control what gets signed — PIXTEL never has access to your private keys.

### Token Holding Benefits

If your wallet holds **10,000+ PIXTEL tokens**, you unlock:
- Ability to create **public rooms**
- Future exclusive features for token holders

## Security

- **Your keys, your crypto** — PIXTEL never stores or has access to your private key
- All transactions require **explicit approval** through Phantom's popup
- The game only reads your **public address** and **token balances**
- Wallet connection can be revoked at any time through Phantom's settings

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Phantom not detected | Make sure the Phantom extension is installed and enabled |
| Connection failed | Refresh the page and try again |
| Transaction stuck | Check Solana network status; try again after a moment |
| Balance not showing | The game retries automatically; wait a few seconds |

## Alternative: Play Without Wallet

If you don't have a Phantom wallet, you can still play PIXTEL with a regular email account. You'll have access to all minigames and social features — only crypto-specific features (token burning, public room creation for holders) require a wallet.
